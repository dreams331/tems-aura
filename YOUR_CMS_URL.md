## 🚀 Quick Start

### 1. Complete Netlify Setup (10 minutes)

Follow these steps in order:

#### A. Go to Netlify
https://app.netlify.com

#### B. Find Your Site
- Look for "tems-aura" in your sites list
- Click on it

#### C. Enable Identity
- Click "Identity" tab
- Click "Enable Identity" button
- Wait for confirmation

#### D. Enable Git Gateway  
- Still in Identity tab
- Scroll to "Services" section
- Click "Enable Git Gateway"
- Confirm it shows as "Enabled"

#### E. Invite Yourself
- Click "Invite users" button
- Enter: `tems_aura@gmail.com`
- Click "Send"

#### F. Accept Invitation
- Check your email inbox
- Open invitation from Netlify
- Click the invitation link
- Set a strong password
- Confirm and submit

---

### 2. Login to Your CMS

1. **Go to:** https://tems-aura.vercel.app/admin/index.html
2. **Click:** "Login with Netlify Identity"
3. **Enter your credentials:**
   - Email: tems_aura@gmail.com
   - Password: [your password from step F]
4. **Click:** "Log in"
5. **🎉 You're in!**

---

### 3. Test Your CMS

Once logged in:

1. Click "Gallery" in left sidebar
2. Click "New Gallery"
3. Fill in:
   - Title: "Test Design"
   - Category: Choose one
   - Upload a test image
   - Description: "Testing CMS"
   - Date: Today
4. Click "Publish"
5. Wait 1-2 minutes
6. Visit your live site gallery
7. ✅ See your test content!

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Can't see login button?
- Hard refresh (Cmd+Shift+R)
- Try incognito mode
- Clear browser cache

### Login button not working?
- Complete Netlify Identity setup first
- Make sure Git Gateway is enabled
- Check you accepted the invitation

### Can't save content?
- Verify Git Gateway enabled in Netlify
- Check internet connection
- Try logging out and back in
